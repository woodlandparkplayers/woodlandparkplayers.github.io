Woodland Park Players

A Burgeoning Theater Group in your 'hood

Our Mission

To provide the opportunity for individual adult members of the Woodland Park community to become involved in the various aspects of the performing arts within a pleasant social atmosphere. To generate funds for the benefit of the Seattle Public Schools arts programs.

Membership

To be involved in Woodland Park Players in any capacity (cast, crew, organizers, volunteers, etc.), you must be a member.
