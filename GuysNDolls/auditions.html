How to prepare for Auditions

To try out for a lead or speaking role, please prepare:

• 60-90 second monologue

• 60 second song (verse and a chorus) please bring sheet music


To try out for an ensemble role, please prepare:

• 60-90 second story or joke to tell

• 60 second song (song of your choice) please bring sheet music. Or if you prefer, you can sing Happy Birthday or Twinkle Twinkle


Who is eligible to audition?

Anyone can audition for a lead or speaking role.

However, the ensemble — which is the bulk of the cast—will be cast with members of the “Woodland Park community.”

The “Woodland Park community” is defined as having one or more of the following:

1) Current or former staff, parent/guardian or former student (over 16) of West Woodland Elementary School

2) Live within the WPP defined Geo-Zone, which is the area:
• south of N. 80th St.
• west of Aurora Ave N/SR-99  
• north of N. 50th St.   
• east of 8th Ave NW
3) Member of WPP’s inaugural cast (2016 Oklahoma!)

What are the available roles and responsibilities of those roles?

Ensemble (minimum 2 scenes, smallest commitment): 

Eligibility

• To be eligible to audition for the ensemble, you must be a member of the “Woodland Park community”, as defined above.

Responsibilities

• Once selected, you must commit to the rehearsal schedule and sign a “commitment statement” at our WPP Mandatory Business Meeting, December 5, 2016.
• Annual Dues: Ensemble members must pay annual family dues of $40 for the first adult member, plus $10 for each additional adult; 3)
• Ticket sales • Ensemble members commit to selling at least 10 tickets (total) for the performances on March 17 and 18 (2 performances).
In the event that we have more “Woodland Park community” members audition than we have parts, final casting decision will be at director’s discretion. In the event you are NOT cast in this year’s production, please know there is a place for you in the Woodland Park Players! We need lots of help behind the scenes---sets, costumes, props, lighting, craft services, ad sales, front of the house, etc.  And if you join the crew and audition again next year, you will be considered with higher priority. 

Supporting Role (4+ scenes, several speaking parts and larger rehearsal commitment)

Eligibility

• Anyone is eligible to audition for a supporting role
• Note: Director will be encouraged to cast a member of the “Woodland Park community” (as defined above) for supporting roles, but ultimate casting decision is at director’s discretion.

Responsibilities of Supporting Role

• Once selected, you must commit to the rehearsal schedule and sign a “commitment statement” at our WPP Business Meeting on December 5, 2106.
• Annual Dues: Supporting Role members must pay annual family dues of $40 for the first adult member, plus $10 for each additional adult.
• Ticket sales: Supporting Role members commit to selling at least 10 tickets (total) for the performances on March 17 and 18 (2 performances).

Lead Role (5+ scenes, major dialogue and maximum of rehearsals)


Eligibility

• Anyone is eligible to audition for a lead role.
• Note: Director will be encouraged to cast a member of the “Woodland Park community” (as defined above) for lead roles, but ultimate casting decision is at director’s discretion.

Responsibilities of Lead Role:

• Once selected, you must commit to the rehearsal schedule and sign a “commitment statement” at our WPP Business Meeting on December 5, 2016.
• Annual Dues: Lead Role members must pay annual family dues of $40 for the first adult member, plus $10 for each additional adult.
• Ticket sales: Lead Role members commit to selling at least 10 tickets (total) for the performances on March 17 and 18 (2 performances).

Who is eligible to be part of the crew?

We gladly welcome anyone to join our crew. With each production, we are in need of:

• Set design and building
• Props
• Costumes
• Craft services
• Ad sales
• Concessions
• Video/photography
• Light and sound
• Make-up crew
• House and publicity
