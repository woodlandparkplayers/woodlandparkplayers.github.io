Audition Information

All Auditions and Callbacks are in West Woodland Cafeteria, 5601 4th Ave NW

General Auditions (Everyone): Saturday, November 19th, 10am-5pm

How to prepare for Auditions

To try out for a lead or speaking role, please prepare:

• 60-90 second monologue

• 60 second song (verse and a chorus) please bring sheet music


To try out for an ensemble role, please prepare:

• 60-90 second story or joke to tell

• 60 second song (song of your choice) please bring sheet music. Or if you prefer, you can sing Happy Birthday or Twinkle Twinkle
